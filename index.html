About Us

Founded in 2015, ZEROHERO Training is the peak body for exercise and sports science in Indonesia. Our mission is to enhance performance, health and well-being through the science of exercise and sport. We are also committed to establishing, promoting and defending the career paths of tertiary trained exercise and sports science practitioners.
